The skill I probably know the most about and the one that I can teach to another person the easiest would most likely be creating CGI in blender.
So let's get started on making a simple scene with God Rays.

Prerequisites

Skills Basic computer literacy
Software Blender 3.0 or later
Hardware Preferably a non-integrated GPU, but a high end integrated GPU is acceptable.

First we will delete all objects in the scene, then add a plane, camera
and position the camera to an angle that is proper to take in the scene, and add a material to the plane.

Then we need to add a cube, then add a material, delete the color and add a volume scatter in the volume area, and set it's density to 0.1.
After that, we'll create a circle, press tab to go into edit mode, e to extrude and scale the circle up to create a small hole. We'll then align the
hole with the light.

We're almost done. Next what we'll need to do is increase the power of the light to about 100,000W, and switch to rendered mode, and look through
camera view, and we're finished. Just take your angle, and boom a completed god ray in Blender.
